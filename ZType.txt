How to play ZType:

1. Launch the game
2. Pink words will starts falling down, choose a word to start typing
3. Once a word turns blue, you can only continue to type that word, look at the falling blue word and start typing them as accurately and quickly as possible. 
4. Type each letter in the word one by one. If you make a mistake, continue to type the correct letter.
5. As you type each letter correctly, the word will start to get shorter. 
6. Once you have typed the entire word correctly, it will be destroyed and you can start typing another word you see on screen.
7.Be careful not to let the words reach the bottom of the screen. If a word reaches the bottom, the game is over.
